Activities We Offer

Quad Biking

If you're an adventure seeker, consider quad biking in the Sahara desert, quad biking is for those who are seeking an exciting and fun day activity close to Marrakech, this tours may take you through different types of landscapes, providing a diverse and exciting experience. Quad biking often includes stops at viewpoints or landmarks within the desert, allowing you to take unforgettable panorama and memorable pictures. Discover the beauty of the country by taking in the stunning and unique views and plenty of desert wildlife.

Sahara Tour

Sahara tours typically include activities such as sand dune exploration, visiting desert oases, witness the stunning desert sunrise and sunset. The colors and the play of light on the sand are truly mesmerizing. The Sahara has become a popular tourist destination, with travellers coming to experience its unique landscapes, cultures, and adventure opportunities, such as camel treks and desert camping. Sahara Desert tour offers a chance to explore one of the world's most iconic and mystical landscapes. so join us for a unforgettable day!

Sandboarding

Sandboarding is an experience that can be enjoyed by people of all ages. Sandboarding in the Sahara is considered an eco-friendly activity since it doesn't require any infrastructure, such as ski lifts or grooming equipment. hile enjoying sandboarding in the Sahara, you can also immerse yourself in the local culture of the desert communities. This includes trying traditional foods, listening to desert music, and interacting with the Berber people who have called the desert home for generations.
